Game Instructions

How to Play

  • Step 1: Prepare a soldier for 0.1ETH
  • Step 2: Wait the required block time.
  • Step 3: Start shooting enemies.
  • Step 4: Collect divs and battle more enemies.

Game Details

  • Prepare a Soldier: 1 soldier with 1 bullet.
  • Block Wait Times:
    • Random Shot: 200 Blocks
    • Targeted Shot: 800 Blocks
  • One soldier dies each time another player shoots.
  • If one of your soldiers die, you keep the bullet from that soldier if they had one remaining.
  • When a soldier dies, divs are given to the survivors and the dead soldier is added to the refund line.
  • One time self-kill protection. If you own more than one soldier, a random shot can kill your own soldiers. Be careful.
  • Div's are disbursed back to wallets automatically.
  • Chat in the discord to strategize. Don't piss off the wrong person!
